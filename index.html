Founder - Sarva Mangalam Charitable Trust : Vivek Kumar Saroj

Sarva Mangalam Charitable Trust was founded by Vivek Kumar Saroj. This Trust is a selfless and sacred effort dedicated to the welfare of the weaker and underprivileged sections of society.
This trust is based on service, compassion, and humanitarian values, with the aim of bringing positive change to society. Under the leadership of Shri Vivek Kumar Saroj, the organization works in vital areas such as education, health, women's empowerment, senior citizen care, and assistance to the poor. The trust aims to reach out to every section of society and meet their needs.
